Stacking context example

Each container creates its own stacking context, so its childrens' z-index will be respected only within the parent, outside of it, any element with z-index greater than parent's z-index will be shown on top of parent's children.

I have z-index: 1

Child paragraph: I have z-index: 999

I have z-index: 2

Child paragraph: I have z-index: 2
